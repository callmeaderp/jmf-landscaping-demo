What We Do

Our Services

From landscape design to hardscape installation, lawn care to snow removal — we handle every aspect of your outdoor space.

Landscape Design & Installation

Custom on-site consultations and full design services. New landscapes or complete overhauls — including hardscaping, pergolas, lighting, and creative plantings.

Paver Patios & Walls

ICPI & NCMA certified installations. Natural stone, permeable pavers, clay, and concrete options. Fire pits, outdoor kitchens, sitting walls, and more.

Lawn Maintenance

Mowing, seasonal cleanups, shrub trimming, garden bed weeding, mulching, and edging. Per-trip or seasonal contracts for residential and commercial properties.

Water Features

Custom streams, waterfalls, and ponds designed as stunning focal points. Includes optional maintenance plans to keep your water features pristine year-round.

Landscape Lighting

Low-voltage LED pathway, focal, patio, and pillar lighting. Transform your property's character and extend your outdoor enjoyment into the evening hours.

Commercial Services

Full commercial maintenance, annual flower programs, and around-the-clock snow plowing and salting. Truck, loader, and bobcat equipment for any scale.

Our Story

Built on Passion,
Grown with Trust

Owner Jason Fowler started his career in 1999 at one of New York's premier garden centers, spending a decade mastering hundreds of plant species and landscape techniques.

In Spring 2005, he turned that expertise into JMF Landscaping — and the company hasn't stopped growing since. What started as a single crew has expanded to four full crews by 2012, with continued year-over-year growth.

Every member of the team pursues ongoing education annually, ensuring your project benefits from the latest industry knowledge and techniques.

Our Specialty

Hardscape
Experts

With over a decade specializing in paver patios and retaining walls, our ICPI and NCMA certified team delivers hardscape installations built to industry standards — and built to last.

Natural Stone, Pavers & Concrete

Permeable pavers, clay, natural stone — we work with every major material and follow ICPI/NCMA guidelines on every installation.

Outdoor Living Features

Fire pits (gas or wood), sitting walls, benches, pillars, steps, outdoor kitchens, and fireplaces to complement any hardscape.

Cleaning, Sealing & Restoration

Professional cleaning and sealing protects against Rochester's climate. Choose wet look, shiny, or matte finishes to restore your patio's beauty.
